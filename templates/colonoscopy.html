# Your Screening option is: a Colonoscopy
A colonoscopy is a medical procedure where a doctor inserts a camera mounted on a colonoscope in order to monitor and find diseases. With a colonoscopy, you **will** need to have someone drive you home, because you will have to be sedated for the procedure. You will also have to refrain from eating before the procedure and take a laxative before the procedure.
